Mount Longonot Hiking Trail

Rising from the floor of the Great Rift Valley in Kenya is the extinct Volcano of Mount Longonot.
It is located south east of lake Naivasha.

Longonot Hiking trail

Hiking to the top of this volcano will be both challenging and rewarding at the same time.

Mount Longonot offers a 3.1 Km hiking trail from the entrance to the rim of the crater.

There is a rest point before the last steep climb to the top of the crater.

Oloonongot Crater peak

At the Oloonongot Crater Peak, you can view the unique thick forest that lies within the crater
of the mountain.

You can also get scenic view across the rift Valley all the way to lake Naivasha.
